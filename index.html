About Me

Hi, I'm Jonas Bronson, a college student majoring in computer science and cybersecurity at Kansas State University. Video production and editing have been a passion of mine since late in my middle school years. I took my first video production course at Shawnee Mission Northwest high school (SMNW) in 2019. During my years there, I completed all of the video production courses and become an Executive Producer for the advanced video production class' video program, KUGR. While in KUGR, I directed and produced videos, ran productions, live-streamed, and did jobs for the school district. During my senior year, I took classes at the Shawnee Mission School District Center for Academic Achievement for cybersecurity and game design.

My video production work has won national awards in 2022 and 2023 from the Journalism Education Association (JEA). In addition, I also do my own work for clients and personal projects. I have an extensive background in computer programming, cybersecurity, game design, and graphic design. I currently work for Wildcat Watch at Kansas State University as a Student Organization Video Producer where I manage others, film and edit videos.
